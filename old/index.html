TATA La Vida Gurgaon

"Tata La Vida" in Sector 113 Gurgaon is full of exquisiteness, stylishness and nature at its best. “La Vida” is a Spanish word that means “Life”, so if we go through the meaning then it is a life of luxury and convenience, which Tata Housing presenting for you in this residential project.

Tata Housing La Vida has been imagined to incorporate a combination of absolute luxury, modern amenities with contemporary infrastructure. Here, providing comfort, convenience and security have been the most excellent design factors, while making sure homes that offer true value. 8 high-rise towers featuring exclusively designed 2 BHK, 2.5 BHK apartments with luxury specifications. A residence which is a calming retreat to forget the pressure of the aggressive world, rest back and wake up to fight with another demanding day.

Tata La Vida luxury structure is spread over 12 acres and consists of 8 towers with wonderfully designed apartments, La Vida has a wonderful location that's peaceful, clean, pollution & noise-free and far from the rush of the city, yet close to every convenience. With 8.875 acres of lush green landscape, the development is a perfect home studded with high-class facilities & amenities, with a deserving price range.

Abundant cross ventilation and daylight in every unit in this development, an inventive merge of contemporary design and, planned for low maintenance cost and wide greens stretching all around are the USPs of this community. The entire project has been perfectly planned with luxury amenities like a clubhouse, gym, swimming pool, security and power back up, etc.
